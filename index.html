Projects

GetLimed.com

I implemented a redesign for LIME Marketing's corporate web presence.  We began with a pile of images and video, some graphic assets and a a rough concept for an animated image gallery.  I bootstrapped the page layout and executed the design over the course of three weeks, producing over 900 lines of JavaScript in the process.

GetLimed.com Mobile

I had already optimized getlimed.com for mobile and iPad, but the gallery concept did not translate well to the smallest screens.  I took cues from the full-sized getlimed.com concept and adapted them for mobile.

Lime CRM

I wrote this custom CRM application for LIME Marketing using the Symfony2 PHP framework.  I took the project from concept to a working prototype in under a week.

Cordell Tribute

Every year for Christmas my family can't decide what to get my dad.  Last year my mom suggested socks.  I suggested a web application reminscent of Kid Pix, but customized to my father's very unique personality. Drawing on the canvas will randomly play audio clips of his catch phrases.

SweetMuffinSuite.com

SweetMuffinSuite.com is a Wordpress site that I built for a friend of my wife's named Pauline Grayson.  I used Pauline's designs to create a custom Wordpress theme for her landing page, blog and portfolio.

MelissaEsplin.com

Melissa is my wife, and I am her 24/7 tech support.  I handle the back end of her blog so she can focus on design.  In the fall of 2011 I migrated MelissaEsplin.com to Amazon EC2 and S3.

VEx (Vault Express)

I cut my PHP teeth on an internal web application for my workgroup at FamilySearch.  I coded the entire thing in Notepad++ and made every mistake.  We used VEx to meet all of our goals and continued to use it every day until the release of VEx 2.0.

VEx 2.0

VEx 2.0 is a complete rewrite of VEx.  I used the CakePHP framework with extensive Javascript/JQuery.  The second go 'round focused on faster, cleaner code, error handling and fixing all of the UX mistakes from VEx 1.0.
