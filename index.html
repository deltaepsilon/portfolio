Hello World

These are some of my web projects.  I started out as a financial data analyst, but after two years of automating my analyses and data manipulations, I'm now a programmer who happens to work with lots of data.  My specialty is still data, but I've extended into data-driven web applications and the attending user experience.  My current project includes loads of data in a fast-paced production environment where success is driven by intuitive UX and efficient queries.  I work side by side in operations with my users, so I hear every complaint and incorporate a steady stream of suggestions into my product.

I work in CakePHP with a heavy dose of JQuery and CoffeeScript.  My recent projects have incorporated node.js and socket.io, Oracle PL/SQL stored procedures, MySQL, canvas data charts, Apache optimizations, Amazon EC2/S3, Linux, Wordpress, elastic layouts and single-page applications.  I'm looking for my first excuse to try out some nosql, and I'd like to do more public-facing work where I get to flex my CSS muscles.

Projects

In 2009 I launched an eCommerce business that is still selling survival equipment today.  I created everything but the graphical elements, which I owe to my wife Melissa.  The redesign will be done by November 2011.

VEx (Vault Express)

I cut my PHP teeth on an internal web application for my workgroup at FamilySearch.  I coded the entire thing in Notepad++ and made every mistake.  We used VEx to meet all of our goals and it's still used in production six months later.

VEx 2.0

VEx 2.0 is a complete rewrite of VEx.  I used the CakePHP framework with extensive Javascript/JQuery.  The second go 'round focused on faster, cleaner code, error handling and fixing all of the UX mistakes from VEx 1.0.
